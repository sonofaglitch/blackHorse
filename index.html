Welcome

Knowing beer is our thing. We offer the finest real ales from Leiecester and further afield for you to enjoy in a relaxed & welcoming setting. Tip your hat to a classic pub, done just as it should be.

Find us

We're right here on Braunstone Gate, in Leicester's vibrant West End. This cosmopolitan area is known for its great bars and restaurants and it's just a stone's throw from the city centre.

What's on

Leicester's best-kept secret for live music, we play host four nights a week. You'll always find something afoot to entertain you here while you get to know our great selection of classic drinks.
